Temiloluwa,
the engineer...

With global warming looming and the world entering a state of panic, the need for environmental engineers is more prominent, now more than ever.
That is where I come in as an engineer. Putting my back to work alongside engineers in other fields, the aim is to counter the human-inflicted damage on our world and make it great again. We need all the hands we can get. That includes yours and mine.

Temiloluwa,
the creative...

Running off to fight battles to prevent the end of the world as we know it is all exciting and good. However,Relaxing by contributing to the creative society is just as exciting!

Between learning a few chords on the guitar, finishing up a DIY project, taking aesthetic photos or writing a poem, there lies my peace of mind.

Pouring out my imagination and sharing to the world via blogposts and occasional status updates. Although the blood of an engineer flows within, I possess the bones of a creative through and through.
